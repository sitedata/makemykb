PayPal is a widely used and accepted payment processor for online payments. To create a PayPal account, simply follow the steps below.

  • Step 1. Visit the PayPal.com website.
  • Step 2. Click the "Sign Up" button.
  • Step 3. Choose between a Personal Account and a Business Account.

    • If you're making transactions on behalf of a business, you should select a business account.

    If you're making transactions for yourself and only yourself then you should select a personal account.

  • Step 4. Click the "Next Button".
  • Step 5. Select your location from the drop-down box.
  • Step 6. Click the "Get Started" button.
