Stripe is an online payment processor that helps customers process payments using credit and debit cards all over the world.

To create a Stripe account, simply follow the steps provided below.

  • Step 1. Visit the https://stripe.com website.
  • Step 2. Click the "Start Now" button on the website introductory page.
  • Step 3. Enter your email, full name, password and then select your country.
  • Step 4. Click the "Create Account" button.

    • You'll receive an email to the address entered at account registration which you'll need to click or enter a code from to verify your email address.
