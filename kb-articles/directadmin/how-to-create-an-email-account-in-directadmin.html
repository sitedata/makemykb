To create a new email account within DirectAdmin, simply perform the following:

  • Step 1.
    • Log in to the DirectAdmin control panel.
  • Step 2.
    • Navigate to the E-Mail subsection and select "E-mail Accounts".
  • Step 3.
    • Click the "Create Account" button.
  • Step 4.
    • Enter your username, password and select a storage quota. Username - The prefix before your domain name in your email address (e.g user@domain.com) Password - The password used to log in to the email account. You can create your own or generate one at random. E-mail Quota - The total storage the email account can use from your package limits.
  • Step 5.
    • Click the "Create Account" button.
